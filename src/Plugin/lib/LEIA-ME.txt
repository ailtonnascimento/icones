COPIE VideoOS.Platform.dll PARA ESTA PASTA
=========================================

Na máquina com XProtect instalado, execute no PowerShell:

  Get-ChildItem "C:\Program Files\Milestone" -Recurse -Filter "VideoOS.Platform.dll" | Select-Object -First 1 FullName

Copie o arquivo encontrado para:

  C:\Cursor\project01\lib\VideoOS.Platform.dll

Depois compile novamente no Visual Studio.
